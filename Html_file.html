Angat Buhay Foundation

Angat Buhay Foundation, founded by FVP Leni Robredo, is a non-governmental/non-profit organization that promotes a large volunteer network by empowering Filipinos with their cause. Its five target pillars are public education, health and food security, climate action, community engagement, and arts and culture. These pillars are what keeps this organization continue to move and grow. Being a volunteer allows you to be one of the people who can help our nation overcome its hardships during disasters and support our fellow citizens in their daily lives.
